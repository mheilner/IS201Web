  1. What is Net Zero?
    • According to Oxford Languages, "Net Zero" is a targer for completely negating the amount of greenhouse gasses produced by a human activity. This is done by both reducting the emmissions produces by the world as well as implementing methods for absorbing carbon dioxide emissions alreading in the atmosphere.

    • Despite the thought that one person can not make a tangible differece, we can each support this important goal by doing our part with the following suggentions!
      • Look at Home Energy Use and Waste Production. This espectially includes consumption of natural gas, electricity, and other fuels. If they can be reduced, do your part!
      • How do you travel? If you can adjust your lifestyle to take more public transportation or use alternative means like a bike or walk, this can significantly reduce your personal footprint.
      • Take a look at what you eat and where these items are sources. The less energy intensive these foods are to produce, the better for the environment.
      • Moderate your use of heating and air conditioning. Alternatively you can dress for the conditions.
      • Take shorter showers!
